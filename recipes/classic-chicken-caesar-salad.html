Classic Chicken Caesar Salad

Green Delight

Ingredients

  • 6 cups tightly packed chopped romaine lettuce
  • 1 pound cooked boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into strips
  • 1/2 cup finely shredded Parmesan cheese, such as Cracker Barrel
  • 1/2 cup seasoned croutons
  • 1/4 cup creamy Caesar dressing, such as Kraft

Steps

  1. Arrange lettuce on four serving plates. Top with chicken, Parmesan, and croutons, then drizzle with dressing.
